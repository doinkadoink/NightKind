Nightkind.org ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

1. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Account Information: if you create an account, we store your login credentials (securely hashed).
  • Order Details: including purchase history, billing, and shipping data.
  • Technical Data: such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited (via cookies or analytics tools, if used).

2. How We Use Your Information

We only use your information for purposes that are clearly communicated, including:

  • Fulfilling and confirming product orders
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending optional marketing emails (only if you've opted in)
  • Creating and managing user accounts (if applicable)
  • Improving website functionality and user experience

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data may be shared only with trusted third-party services as required for:

  • Payment processing
  • Order fulfillment and shipping
  • Website hosting and analytics

These third parties are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the services they provide.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Access or update your account information
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal data by contacting us

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze site traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Nightkind.org is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
